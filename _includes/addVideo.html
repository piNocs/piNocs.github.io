Adding a Video

  • Required Hardware

    • Some way to connect the micro SD card to your computer
    • The micro SD card. Retrieve this from the right side of the Pi
    • The new video, on your computer, named video.mp4 in all lowercase. The code won't work unless the video is named video.mp4

  • Moving the video to the SD card

    Don't forget to eject the SD card before pulling it out!

    Insert the SD card
    Connect the Micro SD to your computer however you see fit. This behaves much like a USB drive. It will appear as if two drives were just inserted; we will be using 7.9 GB Volume(not boot). Below is an image of what the folder's contents look like.

    Navigate to the /home/pi/ folder
    After clicking on 7.9 GB Volume, open the folder named home, then open the folder named pi you should see a file named video.mp4 here. If you want to leave this video on the Pi, rename it to anything but video.mp4. This is okay to do.

    Paste the new video in the pi folder
    Open a different window, find your folder, and copy the video. Go back to the /home/pi window and paste the video in this folder. Make sure the video is named video.mp4 in all lowercase.

  • Safely eject the SD card

    Eject both boot and 7.9 GB Volume. You can do this one of two ways.

    • 1. Left click each item name and select eject.
    • 2. Hit the little up arrow next to each item.
