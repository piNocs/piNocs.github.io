Adding a Video

  • The process

    You will be inserting the SD card into your computer. On OSX and Windows, one partition called boot will appear. On linux, two partitions will appear.

    The video will be loaded onto and played from the partition named boot.

  • Moving the video to the SD card

    Don't forget to eject the SD card before pulling it out!

    Insert the SD card
    Connect the Micro SD to your computer however you see fit. This behaves much like a USB drive. A drive named boot will appear.

    The video is loaded directly into the boot
    After opening boot — if there is a video — rename it to anything but video.mp4.

    Copy the new video in the boot directory
    Open a different window, find your videos's folder, and copy the video. Go back to the boot window and paste the video in this folder. Make sure the video is named video.mp4 in all lowercase.

  • Safely eject the SD card

    Eject boot in one of two ways.

    • 1. Left click each item name and select eject.
    • 2. Hit the little up arrow next to each item.
