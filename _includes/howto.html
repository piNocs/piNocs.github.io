How to use the Pi-noculars

The Raspberry Pi is programmed to run a video on a loop as soon as it is plugged in. A little after closing time, the Pi shuts off on its own.

That's it! Plug it in and it works. To save the screen, the staff at the exhibit should unplug the device from the wall outlet at night. If people are seeing a blue screen, unplug it then plug it back in. If you ever find yourself handling the device, please be gentle.

Only unplug the device if you see a blue screen or it's after closing time! By unplugging it unexpectedly, the SD card could become corrupt and the program will need to be reinstalled. If you find yourself needing to turn off the device, check out the shutdown instructions.
